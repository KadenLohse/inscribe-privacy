# Privacy Policy for Inscribe

*Last updated: April 13, 2026*

Inscribe is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Data Collection

Inscribe does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data on remote servers. We do not have user accounts, analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party data collection of any kind.

## Local Storage

All data you create in the app — including the verses you add, your memorization progress, review history, and settings — is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync, in which case it is stored in your personal iCloud account managed by Apple, not by us.

## Bible Translation Lookups

When you choose to fetch a verse from a free translation, Inscribe makes a request to a public Bible text API to retrieve the verse text. No personal information is sent with this request — only the reference and translation you selected. The verse text is then stored locally on your device, and no further requests are made for that verse.

## Notifications

Inscribe uses local notifications to remind you to review verses throughout the day. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device by iOS. No notification content is sent through any external server.

## Children's Privacy

Inscribe does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
